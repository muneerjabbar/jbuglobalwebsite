KULLIYATH HADITHA comprehensive course designed to provide an in-depth understanding of Hadith sciences. It covers key topics such as the collection, classification, and authentication of Hadith, along with the biographies of prominent Muhaddithun (Hadith scholars). Students will study major Hadith compilations, principles of Hadith criticism, and the application of Hadith in Islamic jurisprudence. This course aims to equip learners with the knowledge and skills to analyze and interpret Hadith in light of Islamic scholarship.
FIQH STUDIESA structured course that explores the principles and rulings of Islamic jurisprudence. It covers essential topics such as the sources of Islamic law (Quran, Hadith, Ijma, and Qiyas), the differences between various schools of thought, and the application of fiqh in contemporary issues. Students will gain a deep understanding of worship (Ibadah), transactions (Mu'amalat), family law, and ethics, enabling them to apply Islamic rulings in daily life with scholarly insight.
